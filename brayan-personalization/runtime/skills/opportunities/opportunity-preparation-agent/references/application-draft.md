# Application-draft mode

Use for short forms where the main leverage is clear answers, proof artifacts, screenshots, and a submission checklist; CV may be secondary or irrelevant.

Primary outputs in `preparation-packet.md`:
- form/source summary
- recommended form choices
- answer drafts with character/word limits when known
- proof-artifact checklist
- screenshot/account/email blockers
- safety/redaction checklist
- manual submission checklist

Optional companion file:
- `application/application-draft.md` when answers are long enough to deserve their own file.

Do not submit the form. Brayan must review and submit unless he explicitly approves the exact external action.
