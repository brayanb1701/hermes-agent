# Grant-proposal mode

Use for grants, compute/API-credit grants, model-access programs, research residencies/fellowships with proposal emphasis, and funding opportunities.

Primary outputs in `preparation-packet.md`:
- funder/program summary
- eligibility and deadline checklist
- project/proposal angle recommendation
- proposal draft or outline
- budget/compute/token estimate
- timeline and milestones
- proof artifacts/screenshots/links needed
- risk/safety/redaction checklist
- manual submission blockers

Optional companion file:
- `application/proposal-draft.md`

Do not invent budget, affiliations, guarantees, or completed proof artifacts. Label assumptions explicitly.
